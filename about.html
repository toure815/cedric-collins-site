His story

Cedric P. Collins attended Capital University Law School while working full time. That experience shaped how he practices today: he understands what it means to carry a lot at once, and he meets every client with patience rather than pressure.

He earned his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science from Wittenberg University in Springfield, Ohio, was admitted to the State of Ohio Bar in November 2012, and to the United States District Court for the Southern District of Ohio in 2013.

How he serves the community

Beyond representing his own clients, Cedric is appointed by the courts as a Guardian ad Litem on both the Abuse, Neglect, and Dependency docket and the Juvenile and Domestic Custody docket in Franklin, Fairfield, and Licking Counties. That work gives him a rare, firsthand view of how Ohio judges actually weigh decisions about children, insight he brings to every custody and divorce matter he handles.
