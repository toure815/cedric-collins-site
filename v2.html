Questions families ask first.

Yes. Your first conversation is free and completely confidential. You will leave knowing where you stand and what your options are, whether or not you decide to work together.

What areas of Ohio do you serve?+

The office is in Pickerington and serves the greater Columbus area, including Franklin, Fairfield, and Licking Counties, where attorney Collins is also appointed as a Guardian ad Litem.

Every divorce is different, and cost depends on whether your case is contested and how complex the issues are. Your first consultation is free and includes an honest picture of likely costs.

Yes. Custody, parenting time, and your children’s best interest are a core focus, informed by attorney Collins’s work as a court-appointed Guardian ad Litem.
